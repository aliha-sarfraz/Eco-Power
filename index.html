Eco-Power

Transforming trees into usable energy for rural communities.

How It Works

1. Trees in forests fall naturally due to storms

2. Fallen trees are collected for wood

3. Wood is burned with little oxygen (pyrolysis)

4. The byproducts (syngas & heat) are captured to generate electricity and power microgrids
